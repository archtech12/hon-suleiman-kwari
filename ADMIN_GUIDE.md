## üìù Managing Content

### **1. News Management** (`/admin/news`)

**Add News Article:**

1. Click **"+ Add News"** button
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Article headline
   - **Excerpt**: Short summary (1-2 sentences)
   - **Content**: Full article text
   - **Category**: Select type (Political Update, Community Event, etc.)
   - **Image URL**: Link to article image
   - **Status**: Published or Draft
3. Click **"Add News"**

**Edit/Delete:**

- Click **"Edit"** to modify an article
- Click **"Delete"** to remove (confirm when prompted)

---

### **2. Projects Management** (`/admin/projects`)

**Add Project:**

1. Click **"+ Add Project"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Project name
   - **Description**: What the project is about
   - **Category**: Infrastructure, Education, Healthcare, etc.
   - **Status**: Completed, Ongoing, Planned
   - **Year**: When the project was/will be done
   - **Image URL**: Project photo link
3. Click **"Add Project"**

**Edit/Delete:**

- Use **"Edit"** or **"Delete"** buttons on each project

---

### **3. Legislative Work** (`/admin/legislative`)

**Add Legislative Item:**

1. Click **"+ Add Legislative"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Bill or motion name
   - **Description**: Details
   - **Type**: Bill, Motion, Committee Work, etc.
   - **Date**: When it was introduced
3. Click **"Add Legislative"**

---

### **4. Media Gallery** (`/admin/media`)

**Add Media:**

1. Click **"+ Add Media"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Photo/video description
   - **URL**: Link to image or video
   - **Type**: Photo or Video
   - **Category**: Event, Campaign, Community, etc.
3. Click **"Add Media"**

---

### **5. About Page** (`/admin/about`)

**Update About Content:**

1. Edit the text in the editor
2. Write about:
   - Background and biography
   - Political career
   - Achievements
   - Vision and mission
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

---

### **6. Constituency Services** (`/admin/constituency`)

Manage information about services provided to constituents:

- Education programs
- Healthcare initiatives
- Infrastructure development
- Youth empowerment

---

### **7. Contact Messages** (`/admin/contact`)

**View Messages:**

- See all messages from the contact form
- Click to view full details
- Respond to important inquiries

---

### **8. Volunteers** (`/admin/volunteers`)

**Manage Volunteer Applications:**

- View all volunteer submissions
- Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
- Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"** to update status
- View volunteer details and contact information

## üö® Troubleshooting

### **Can't Login?**

- Check email and password spelling
- Clear browser cache: `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact system administrator

### **Changes Not Showing?**

- Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
- Hard refresh: `Ctrl + Shift + R`
- Clear browser cache

### **Upload Issues?**

- Ensure image URLs are valid
- Use HTTPS links only
- Check image is publicly accessible
